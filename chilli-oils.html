Artisan Chilli Oils

Cold-Pressed Base

Made with premium cold-pressed extra virgin olive oil that enhances the natural flavors of our chillies. The quality of the base oil is crucial to the final product's excellence.

Slow Infusion Process

Our chillies are slowly infused over several weeks to extract maximum flavor and heat intensity. This traditional method ensures a well-balanced, complex flavor profile.
